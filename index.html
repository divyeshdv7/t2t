About Talk2Trend

Building the Future of Fashion Commerce

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Talk2Trend emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize the way people discover and shop for fashion online. We recognized that modern consumers deserve more than just a transactional shopping experience—they deserve a platform that understands their style, values their time, and delivers exceptional quality.

What started as a small team of fashion enthusiasts and tech innovators has grown into a thriving e-commerce platform serving over 50,000+ satisfied customers across India. Our journey has been defined by our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

At Talk2Trend, we're on a mission to democratize fashion by making high-quality, trending styles accessible to everyone. We believe that great fashion shouldn't come with a premium price tag or complicated shopping experiences. Our platform combines:

  • Curated Collections: Every product is handpicked by our style experts to ensure it meets our strict quality and trend standards.
  • Affordable Luxury: We work directly with manufacturers to eliminate middlemen and pass savings directly to you.
  • Seamless Experience: From browsing to checkout, we've designed every interaction to be intuitive and delightful.
  • Customer First: Your satisfaction isn't just our priority—it's our obsession.

What Sets Us Apart

In a crowded e-commerce landscape, Talk2Trend stands out through:

Quality Assurance

Every product undergoes rigorous quality checks before reaching our warehouse. We partner only with trusted manufacturers who share our commitment to excellence. Our quality guarantee means you can shop with complete confidence.

Innovation-Driven

We leverage cutting-edge technology to enhance your shopping experience. From AI-powered recommendations to voice-controlled navigation, we're constantly innovating to make fashion more accessible and enjoyable.

Sustainable Practices

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint. Our packaging is eco-friendly, we minimize waste throughout our supply chain, and we partner with brands that share our sustainability values.

Community Focus

We're more than a store—we're a community of fashion enthusiasts. Through our blog, social media, and customer engagement initiatives, we foster a vibrant community where style inspiration flows freely.

Our Team

Talk2Trend is powered by a diverse team of fashion experts, technology specialists, customer service professionals, and logistics wizards. United by our passion for fashion and commitment to excellence, we work tirelessly to ensure every customer has an exceptional experience.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency.
  • Excellence: We strive for perfection in everything we do.
  • Innovation: We embrace change and continuously improve.
  • Customer Obsession: Your happiness drives every decision we make.
  • Inclusivity: Fashion is for everyone, and so is our platform.

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow, our focus remains unchanged: providing you with the best fashion shopping experience possible. We're investing in new technologies, expanding our product range, and enhancing our services to serve you better.

Thank you for being part of the Talk2Trend journey. Together, we're not just following trends—we're setting them.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

At Talk2Trend, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a purchase from us.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register, place an order, or interact with our services, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number, shipping address)
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment partners)
  • Account credentials (username and password)
  • Order history and shopping preferences
  • Communication preferences and feedback

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, clickstream data)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Location information (with your consent)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and customer service
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account
  • Communication: To send order confirmations, updates, and marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Personalization: To provide personalized product recommendations and shopping experiences
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other security incidents
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our website, products, and services
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our business (payment processors, shipping companies, marketing platforms)
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or sale of assets
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data (SSL/TLS protocols)
  • Secure payment processing through PCI-DSS compliant partners
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Employee training on data protection practices

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to certain processing of your information

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@talk2trend.com

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve website performance and functionality
  • Deliver targeted advertising

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers to Common Questions

Orders & Shipping

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history in the Dashboard.

What are the shipping charges?

We offer FREE shipping on all orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, a flat shipping charge of ₹99 applies across India.

How long will delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. Metro cities usually receive orders within 3-4 days, while remote areas may take 5-7 days.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding to international markets soon. Subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated!

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day hassle-free return policy. You can return any product within 7 days of delivery if you're not satisfied. The product must be unused, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached.

How do I initiate a return?

Log into your account, go to 'My Orders', select the order you want to return, and click 'Request Return'. Our team will arrange a pickup within 2-3 business days.

When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned product. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

Can I exchange a product?

Yes! If you need a different size or color, you can exchange the product. Select 'Exchange' instead of 'Return' when initiating the request. Exchange is subject to product availability.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Credit Cards, Debit Cards, Net Banking, UPI, Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay), and Cash on Delivery (COD) for orders below ₹5,000.

Is it safe to use my credit/debit card?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption and our payment gateway is PCI-DSS compliant. We never store your card details on our servers.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it's shipped. Go to 'My Orders' and click 'Cancel Order'. If the order has been shipped, you'll need to wait for delivery and initiate a return.

Account & Orders

Do I need an account to place an order?

While you can browse products without an account, you need to register or login to place an order. Creating an account helps you track orders, save addresses, and get personalized recommendations.

How do I reset my password?

Click on 'Login', then select 'Forgot Password'. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Unfortunately, orders cannot be modified once placed. However, you can cancel the order (if not shipped) and place a new one with the correct items.

Products & Sizing

How do I choose the right size?

Each product page has a detailed size chart. Click on 'Size Guide' near the size selection to view measurements. If you're still unsure, contact our customer support for assistance.

Are the product colors accurate?

We try our best to display accurate colors. However, actual colors may vary slightly due to screen settings and lighting conditions during photography.

Do you restock sold-out items?

Yes, popular items are regularly restocked. Click 'Notify Me' on sold-out product pages to receive an email when the item is back in stock.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us. Our customer support team is always ready to help!

Shipping & Returns Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Shipping Policy

Shipping Areas

We currently deliver to all serviceable PIN codes across India. During checkout, enter your PIN code to verify if we deliver to your location.

Shipping Charges

  • FREE Shipping: On all orders ₹999 and above
  • Standard Shipping: ₹99 for orders below ₹999
  • Express Shipping: ₹199 (available in select metro cities for next-day delivery)

Delivery Time

Once your order is confirmed and shipped, delivery times are:

  • Metro Cities: 3-4 business days
  • Tier 2 Cities: 4-5 business days
  • Tier 3 Cities & Rural Areas: 5-7 business days
  • Express Delivery: Next business day (select cities only)

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary during sale periods, festivals, or due to unforeseen circumstances.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 24-48 hours of placement. You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately after checkout and a shipping confirmation with tracking details once your order is dispatched.

Order Tracking

Track your order in real-time:

  1. Log into your Talk2Trend account
  2. Go to 'My Orders' in your Dashboard
  3. Click on the order you want to track
  4. View real-time tracking updates

You'll also receive tracking updates via SMS and email at every stage of delivery.

Shipping Partners

We partner with reliable courier services including BlueDart, Delhivery, and India Post to ensure safe and timely delivery of your orders.

Returns & Exchange Policy

Return Window

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within this period.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused, unworn, and unwashed
  • In original condition with all tags attached
  • In original packaging with invoice
  • Returned within 7 days of delivery

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Innerwear, lingerie, and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Products damaged due to misuse or negligence
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Personalized or customized products

How to Return

  1. Log into your account and go to 'My Orders'
  2. Select the order containing the item you want to return
  3. Click 'Request Return' and select the reason
  4. Submit your request
  5. Our team will review and approve your request within 24 hours
  6. Once approved, we'll arrange a pickup from your address within 2-3 business days
  7. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  8. Hand it over to our delivery partner during pickup

Return Shipping

Return pickup is FREE for all eligible returns. We'll arrange the pickup at no additional cost to you.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item:

  1. Our quality team inspects the product (1-2 business days)
  2. If approved, refund is initiated (1 business day)
  3. Amount is credited to your original payment method (5-7 business days)

Refund Timeline:

  • Bank Transfers/Cards: 5-7 business days
  • UPI/Wallets: 3-5 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Refund via bank transfer (please provide account details)

Exchange Policy

Want a different size or color? We've got you covered!

Exchange Process:

  1. Follow the same process as returns, but select 'Exchange' instead
  2. Choose the size/color you want to exchange for
  3. We'll arrange pickup of the original item
  4. New item ships within 24 hours of receiving the returned product

Exchange Shipping: FREE pickup and FREE delivery of the new item

Important: Exchanges are subject to product availability. If the desired item is out of stock, we'll process a refund instead.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  1. Don't accept the delivery if the package appears damaged
  2. If you've already accepted, contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  3. Email photos of the damaged item and packaging to returns@talk2trend.com
  4. We'll arrange immediate pickup and send a replacement at no cost

Wrong Item Delivered

If you receive the wrong item:

  • Contact us immediately at orders@talk2trend.com
  • We'll arrange pickup and send the correct item
  • No charges for return or replacement

Cancellation Policy

Before Shipping

You can cancel your order free of charge before it's shipped:

  1. Go to 'My Orders'
  2. Select the order to cancel
  3. Click 'Cancel Order'
  4. Refund will be processed within 3-5 business days

After Shipping

Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled. You can refuse delivery or initiate a return after receiving the item.
